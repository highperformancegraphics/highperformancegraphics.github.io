Registration

Registered but haven’t received the Ohyay invitation? Please contact the HPG Registrar

With the conference taking place online, registration is simpler than in previous years and only features the following two options:

Free attendance: Watch for free on Youtube or Twitch, links are on the program page
Premium registration : link

Free attendance ($0)

  • Watch the technical paper and poster presentations, panels, and keynotes live
  • Replay them on-demand afterwards; the videos will be made available shortly after the presentation takes place (subject to presenter approval)

Premium registration ($15)

Premium registration additionally grants:

  • Ability to take part in interactive Q&A sessions with the speakers and during panels, as well as be invited to the town hall
  • Access to social and networking online spaces during the conference
  • Receive physical collateral
