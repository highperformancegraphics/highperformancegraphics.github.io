Registration

With the conference taking place online, registration is simpler than in previous years and only features the following two options:

Free registration
Premium registration

Free attendance ($0)

  • Watch the technical paper and poster presentations, panels, and keynotes live
  • Replay them on-demand afterwards; the videos will be made available within 24 hours after the presentation takes place
  • Access to preprints of the papers

Premium ($15)

Premium registering additionally grants:

  • Ability to take part in interactive Q&A sessions with the speakers and during panels, as well as be invited to the town hall
  • Receive physical collateral (register before June 15th to maximize the chance of receiving it before the conference starts).
