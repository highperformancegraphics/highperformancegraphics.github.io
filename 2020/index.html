High-Performance Graphics 2020

High-Performance Graphics was founded in 2009 to synthesize and broaden two important and well-respected conferences in computer graphics: Graphics Hardware and Interactive Ray Tracing

Thank you everybody for contributing and attending HPG 2020! Links to the conference content are on the program page.

Next year’s HPG will be co-located with the Eurographics Symposium on Rendering and will take place in Saarbrücken, Germany next summer.

If you want to engage in the organization of HPG 2021, please contact us!
