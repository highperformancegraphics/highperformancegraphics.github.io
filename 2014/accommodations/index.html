Accommodations

This year there are no specific hotels associated with the conference, however there are a wide variety of hotels with different price points near to the venue. Additional options are available with easy access to the venue using the public transportation system. Some representative options can be seen on this map provided by Eurographics Symposium on Rendering, and you can book a room directly with the hotels or through your standard travel agency supplier.
