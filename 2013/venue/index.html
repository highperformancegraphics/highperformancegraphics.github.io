Venue

High-Performance Graphics will take place in room ‘Pacific A’ of the Hilton Anaheim hotel (on the 2nd floor).

Note that there is no requirement that you book a room in the Hilton Anaheim to attend. The plethora of hotel options, with discounted rates for conference attendees, are available through SIGGRAPH 2013’s travel partner, as the two conferences are co-located. Other registration methods may not receive the same special rates available through SIGGRAPH’s partner.
