Accommodations

High-Performance Graphics 2013 will take place at the Hilton Anaheim hotel, however there are many hotels in the vicinity which provide reasonable accommodation. We recommend attendees book rooms through SIGGRAPH 2013’s travel partner, in order to guarantee discounted rates.

Contact Information

For further information regarding accommodations please contact: general@highperformancegraphics.org.
