Support

Corporate Support

High Performance Graphics is a highly visible, international forum, with established roots, for exchanging experience and knowledge related to computer graphics algorithms, hardware, and systems. The event offers a unique perspective on graphics by combining discussions and constructive critique of innovative concepts as well as product-level designs. It is an inclusive forum for the entire graphics community and brings together researchers, engineers, and architects.

Graphics Hardware and Interactive Ray Tracing, whose synthesis has germinated in the creation of High Performance graphics, had always enjoyed generous corporate support to provide a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere during the conference banquet and lunches, to allow low-cost student registrations, and to provide the best paper prize.

Supporting the conference demonstrates leadership and good will in the graphics community and provides an opportunity to recruit among the influential group of researchers, architects, engineers, and students who attend the conference.

We invite and welcome corporate support in the categories outlined below.

Nurturer $8000

Benefits include:

  • Option to be recognized as supporter of workshop banquet, workshop lunches, or other agreed upon workshop event(s) or feature(s).
  • Most prominent recognition in electronic and printed materials.
  • Most prominent recognition on signage at the event.
  • Option to display a banner with the corporate logo at the event.
  • Option to display products / demos during lunches, or coffee breaks, or the banquet.
  • Option to provide advertisement material that is put into the delegate packs handed out to every participant

Benefactor $5000

Benefits include:

  • Prominent recognition in electronic and printed materials..
  • Prominent recognition on signage at the event.
  • Option to display a banner with the corporate logo at the event.
  • Option to provide advertisement material that is put into the delegate packs handed out to every participant

Supporter $2500

Benefits include:

  • Recognition in electronic and printed materials.
