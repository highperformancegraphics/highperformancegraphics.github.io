Support

High-Performance Graphics is a highly visible, international forum, with established roots, for exchanging experience and knowledge related to computer graphics algorithms, hardware, and systems. The event offers a unique perspective on graphics by combining discussions and constructive critique of innovative concepts as well as product-level designs.

Your contributions are specifically used to acquire the necessary audio/visual services, provide a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere during the conference banquet and lunches, to allow low-cost student registrations, and to provide the best paper prize.

Supporting the conference demonstrates leadership and good will in the graphics community and provides an opportunity to recruit among the influential group of researchers, architects, engineers, and students who attend the conference.

We invite and welcome corporate support in the categories outlined below.

Nurturer $8000

Benefits include:

  • A dedicated 5 minutes of the program for an attendee from your company to promote technical material, and events of interest to an audience of industrial and academic researchers. We expect to see sponsors presenting a preview of their SIGGRAPH talk, technical sessions, and booth experiences, but alerting the audience to your other researcher focused events is another great example use of this time.
  • One complimentary conference registration.
  • The opportunity to include static promotional information that will rotate on the main projectors before and after every session.
  • Most prominent recognition in electronic and printed materials.
  • Most prominent recognition on signage at the event.
  • Option to display a banner with the corporate logo at the event.
  • Option to display products / demos during lunches, or coffee breaks, or the banquet.
  • Option to provide advertisement material that is put into the delegate packs handed out to every participant.

Not For Profit Organizations

Qualified organizations such as universities, research institutes, membership organizations, and consortiums may be entitled to the benefits of a level described above for a reduced rate. Please contact the conference treasurer to determine if your organization is eligible.
