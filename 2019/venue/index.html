Venue

High Performance Graphics 2019 will be held in Strasbourg, France. It is colocated with EGSR19. It will take place at the University of Strasbourg (Esplanade) in the ATRIUM building, 16 Rue René Descartes. Here you can find more information about the different places. Please refer to the venue section on EGSR19 for more information about traveling and getting around in the city.
