In-Person Reception and Keynote

Although HPG is a virtual conference in 2022, we will be hosting an in-person buffet dinner for attendees on the evening of Sunday 7th of August 2022 (the day before SIGGRAPH starts).

The event will be held in the Harbour Centre in downtown Vancouver. There will be an opportunity to dine and mingle with fellow attendees from 5pm in the Harbour Centre Concourse, and there is a dedicated keynote talk and Q&A session (speaker to be announced shortly!) from 7pm in the Fletcher Challenge Theatre.

Please register, and we look forward to seeing you there!
