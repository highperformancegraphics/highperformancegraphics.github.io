In-Person Reception and Keynote

Although HPG is a virtual conference in 2022, we will be hosting an in-person buffet dinner for attendees on the evening of Sunday 7th of August 2022 (the day before SIGGRAPH starts).

The event will be held in the Harbour Centre in downtown Vancouver. There will be an opportunity to dine and mingle with fellow attendees from 5pm in Simon Fraser University's Harbour Centre Concourse.

HPG 2022 In-Person Event Keynote: The Journey to Nanite

Brian Karis — engineering fellow, Epic Games
7pm, August 7 2022, Fletcher Challenge Theatre, Harbour Centre, Simon Fraser University

Speaker bio

Brian Karis is an Engineering Fellow in graphics at Epic Games. Most recently he has led the development of Nanite for UE5. Before this he was most known for work on physically based shading and temporal anti-aliasing although has touched most areas of real-time computer graphics throughout his career. Prior to Epic he worked at Human Head Studios.

Please register, and we look forward to seeing you there!
