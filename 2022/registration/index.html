Registration

With the conference taking place online, registration is simpler than in previous years and only features the following three options:

  • Free attendance: Watch on Youtube or Twitch
  • Premium registration
  • Premium registration and in-person reception and keynote

Free attendance ($0)

  • Watch the technical paper and poster presentations, panels, and keynotes live
  • Replay them on-demand afterwards; the videos will be made available shortly after the presentation takes place (subject to presenter approval)

Premium registration ($15)

Premium registration additionally grants:

  • Ability to take part in interactive Q&A sessions with the speakers and during panels, as well as be invited to the town hall
  • Access to social and networking online spaces during the conference
  • Receive physical collateral

All contributors (i.e. paper/poster authors, presenters, etc.) receive free premium (online) registration

Premium registration and in-person reception and keynote ($40, or $55 after July 14)

While the conference is virtual this year, we are excited to have an optional in-person meet-up in evening of Sunday Aug 7, co-located with SIGGRAPH 2022 in Vancouver. Registering for this grants:

  • Meet fellow HPG attendees in person
  • Enjoy a dedicated keynote, followed by an interactive Q&A, only for reception attendees
  • Buffet meal is provided, dietary options including vegetarian will be available
  • The event is subject to local COVID-19 restrictions for in-person events

Refunds

Full refund available if requested before July 3rd, 2022; no refunds after July 24th, 2022

Questions

Registered but have not received the Ohyay invitation? Please contact the HPG Registrar.
