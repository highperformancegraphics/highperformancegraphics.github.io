Venue

High-Performance Graphics 2015 will be held in Los Angeles, California in the Bunkerhill / Museum room at the Omni Los Angeles Hotel at California Plaza.

We encourage you to stay at The Omni, but attendees are not required to do so. HPG 2015 is co-located with SIGGRAPH 2015. The list of available hotels near and including The Omni, and their discounted rates, is available through SIGGRAPH 2015’s travel planner.
